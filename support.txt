COMMON QUESTIONS

How do I create a habit?
Tap the + button on the home screen and fill out the form.

How do I mark a day complete?
Tap on any goal card and toggle the days you completed.

How are streaks calculated?
Streaks count consecutive weeks where you hit your target frequency.
